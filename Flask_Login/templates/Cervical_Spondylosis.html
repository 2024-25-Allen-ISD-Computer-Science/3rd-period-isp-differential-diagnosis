You have: Cervical Spondylosis

😄

Symptoms:

  • Neck pain
  • Stiffness in the neck
  • Headaches
  • Weakness or numbness in the arms
  • Dizziness or balance problems

Treatment:

Physical therapy, pain relievers, anti-inflammatory medications, and in severe cases, surgery may be considered.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
