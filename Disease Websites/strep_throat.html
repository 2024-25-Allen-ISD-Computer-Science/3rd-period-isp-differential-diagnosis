You have: Strep Throat

😄

Symptoms:

  • Sore throat
  • Pain when swallowing
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Fever
  • White patches on tonsils
  • Headache

Treatment:

Antibiotics such as penicillin or amoxicillin are prescribed. Pain relievers and throat lozenges can help alleviate discomfort.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
