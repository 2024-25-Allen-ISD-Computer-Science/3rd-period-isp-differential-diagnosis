You have: Peptic Ulcer Disease

😄

Symptoms:

  • Burning stomach pain
  • Bloating
  • Indigestion
  • Frequent burping
  • Loss of appetite
  • Dark or tarry stools

Treatment:

Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), H2 blockers, antibiotics (if caused by H. pylori), antacids, and lifestyle changes like avoiding spicy foods.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
