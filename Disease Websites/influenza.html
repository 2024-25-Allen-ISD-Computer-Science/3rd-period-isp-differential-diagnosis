You have: Influenza (Flu)

😄

Symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Muscle aches
  • Fatigue
  • Cough
  • Sore throat

Treatment:

Rest, hydration, and over-the-counter medications such as ibuprofen or acetaminophen can help relieve symptoms. Antiviral medications may be prescribed in some cases.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
