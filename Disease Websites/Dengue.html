You have: Dengue

😄

Symptoms:

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Skin rash
  • Bleeding gums or nosebleeds
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

There is no specific antiviral treatment. Supportive care includes hydration, fever control, and pain relief. Avoid aspirin or NSAIDs.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
