You have: GERD

😄

Symptoms:

  • Heartburn
  • Regurgitation of food or sour liquid
  • Chest pain
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Chronic cough
  • Sore throat

Treatment:

Antacids, proton pump inhibitors, H2 blockers, lifestyle changes (e.g., avoiding certain foods, weight loss, elevating head during sleep).

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
