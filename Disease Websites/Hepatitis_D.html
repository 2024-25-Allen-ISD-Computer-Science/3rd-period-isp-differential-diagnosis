You have: Hepatitis D

😄

Symptoms:

  • Jaundice
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea
  • Swelling in the abdomen
  • Dark urine
  • Loss of appetite

Treatment:

Interferon-alpha therapy is the main treatment. Preventive vaccination against Hepatitis B also helps.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
