You have: Migraine

😄

Symptoms:

  • Severe headache
  • Nausea
  • Sensitivity to light
  • Sensitivity to sound
  • Visual disturbances (aura)
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

Pain relievers, hydration, and rest in a dark, quiet room. Prescription medications may be required for severe cases.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
