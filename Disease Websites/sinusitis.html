You have: Sinus Infection (Sinusitis)

😄

Symptoms:

  • Facial pain
  • Nasal congestion
  • Headache
  • Postnasal drip
  • Cough
  • Fever

Treatment:

Saline nasal sprays, decongestants, and pain relievers can help. If bacterial, antibiotics may be needed.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
