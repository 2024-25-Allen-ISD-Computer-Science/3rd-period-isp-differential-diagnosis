You have: Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)

😄

Symptoms:

  • Burning sensation when urinating
  • Frequent urination
  • Cloudy or strong-smelling urine
  • Pelvic pain
  • Fever
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

Antibiotics prescribed by a doctor. Drinking plenty of water and avoiding irritants like caffeine can help.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
