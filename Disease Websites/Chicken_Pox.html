You have: Chicken Pox

😄

Symptoms:

  • Itchy red spots (rash)
  • Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of appetite
  • Body aches
  • Blister-like sores

Treatment:

Antihistamines for itching, fever-reducing medications, and antiviral drugs for severe cases. Stay hydrated and rest.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
